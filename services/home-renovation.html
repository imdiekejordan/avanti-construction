FAQ

Bathroom Renovation FAQs

What bathroom renovation services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive bathroom renovation services including shower and tub installation, vanity installation, tile work, plumbing, electrical, lighting, flooring, and complete bathroom remodeling. We handle both partial and full bathroom renovations.

How long does a bathroom renovation take?

A typical bathroom renovation takes 2-4 weeks, depending on the scope of work. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we offer professional bathroom design services. Our designers will help you create a functional and beautiful bathroom that meets your needs and preferences, including layout planning, fixture selection, and material coordination.

What materials do you work with?

We work with a wide range of materials including various types of tile, stone, quartz, granite, glass, and more. We'll help you choose materials that match your style, budget, and practical needs for your bathroom.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate with inspectors throughout the renovation process. We ensure all work meets local building codes and regulations, giving you peace of mind.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details before we begin your project.
